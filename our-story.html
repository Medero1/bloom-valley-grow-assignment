Our Story

Greenscape has been beautifying yards and outdoor spaces in our community for over 12 years. We are a family-owned business founded by brothers Tom and Mike Rivera, who grew up working in their father's garden and turned that passion into a full-time career. Today we have a team of 10 trained professionals ready to help you transform your property.

Our Mission

We believe every property deserves to look its best. Our goal is to provide high-quality landscaping services at honest, affordable prices — with friendly service and reliable results. We treat every yard like it is our own.
